About Us

Welcome to Shivam Techno – Empowering Futures, Building Careers.

Established in the year 2001, Shivam Techno has been at the forefront of education, nurturing minds and guiding aspirations for over two decades. With an illustrious history spanning 20 years, Shivam Techno has evolved into a premier training center dedicated to excellence in education.

At Shivam Techno, we believe that knowledge is the key to unlocking potential. Our learning center, nestled in the heart of Aliganj, Lucknow, is more than just a place of education – it's a haven for aspiring individuals seeking to delve into the world of web development and beyond. Shivam Techno is not just an educational institution; we are a community of passionate educators, industry professionals, and enthusiastic learners. More than just technical skills; we offer a pathway to a fulfilling career.






Our Journey

In the ever-evolving landscape of education, Shivam Techno commenced its journey in 2001 with a vision to bridge the gap between knowledge and success. What started as an offline training institute has grown into a beacon of learning, touching the lives of thousands of students.
